You can select a certificate, and then click **View Certificate Details**. The certificate will be displayed in a new pane. In the **Certificate** pane, you can view the General certificate information. Click the **Details** tab to display detailed information about the certificate, such as subject name and subject alternative name. The Certification Path will display the path of the "chain of trust" for the certificate. When you are finished reviewing the certificate information, click **OK**.</span></span>
