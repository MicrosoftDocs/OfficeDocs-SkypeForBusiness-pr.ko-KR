재해 복구를 위해 환경을 준비하는 데 대한 자세한 내용은 [Plan for high availability and disaster recovery in 비즈니스용 Skype 서버.](../../../plan-your-deployment/high-availability-and-disaster-recovery/high-availability-and-disaster-recovery.md)
